The login and registration pages are completed and can be run in node.js.
Implementation functions: 
1. After registration, the data will be transferred to the database in json format. The json includes uuid, username, mail, and hashed password.
2. After logging in, you will jump to another success.ejs page. When the email address is admin@admin.com, you will jump to the admin.ejs page.
3. After modifying the cookie, you will return to the login page.
